Sharpeners

Knives are our constant kitchen companions, but it's amazing how quickly they lose their edge. Not surprising when you think of the jobs they're expected to do. All that cutting, chopping, slicing and dicing, peeling and paring is bound to take its toll. A blunt knife requires more pressure to cut and as a result is harder to control. Thankfully restoring your blades back to their former glory doesn't need any skill, just a good knife sharpener, knife steel or whetstone.
